Energy Audit & Power Sizing Guide

Get the right energy setup for your home or business. Whether you're looking to power a small apartment or a large commercial space, we'll help you calculate your total energy needs - so you only buy what you need, nothing more.

For Homeowners (Domestic Audit)

Ideal for homes, small apartments, and off-grid cabins.

How it works:
  • Download the Energy Audit Spreadsheet (Home Version)
  • List all your appliances (fans, TVs, bulbs, etc.) with their watt ratings
  • The spreadsheet auto-calculates your total energy usage
  • Return the file to us or use the total to shop the right kit in our store

For Businesses (Commercial Audit)

Tailored for offices, shops, warehouses, farms, schools, and larger facilities.

How it works:
  • Download the Commercial Energy Audit Sheet
  • List machines, lighting systems, computers, ACs, etc.
  • The sheet gives you your peak load + total daily consumption
  • Our team can recommend the best system based on your usage
