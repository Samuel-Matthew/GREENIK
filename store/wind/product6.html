Product Description

The 3-Blade Replacement Set is designed for small to mid-sized wind turbines, made from durable nylon fiber reinforced with glass for superior strength and flexibility. These aerodynamic blades provide efficient rotation, low noise, and long-term resistance to weather, making them ideal replacements for damaged or aging blades in residential or mobile wind systems.

Product Specifications

  • Blade Quantity: 3 pieces per set
  • Material: Nylon Fiber (UV and corrosion resistant)
  • Blade Length: ~60cm - 80cm (model dependent)
  • Compatible Turbine Power: 200W - 800W
  • Mounting Hole: Pre-drilled (universal 6 or 8-hole flange)
  • Color: Black / White (varies by stock)

  • Durability: UV-resistant, corrosion-proof, anti-warping
