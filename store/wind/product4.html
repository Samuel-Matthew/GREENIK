Product Description

The Wind Charge Controller (12V/24V, Waterproof) is a smart, weather-resistant unit that regulates the power from a wind turbine to safely charge 12V or 24V battery banks. It automatically prevents overcharging, protects from overcurrent, and diverts excess power when needed. Ideal for small off-grid wind systems, this controller ensures efficiency and battery longevity in all weather conditions.
